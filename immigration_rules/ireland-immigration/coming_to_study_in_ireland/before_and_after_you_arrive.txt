In the sections below we will explain what you have to do before you come to Ireland and what you need to do after you arrive in the country.

## What do I need to do before arriving?

Please ensure you have registered with a college and applied for a visa if required. Please click for further information.

## What do I need to do after arriving?

You must present your documents to an immigration officer. Please click for further information.
