## Introduction

If you want to study in Ireland for longer than 90 days, you must choose a course from theInterim List of Eligible Programmes (ILEP). The following types of course are listed:

- English Language programmes lasting at least 25 weeks

- Higher Education programmes from level 6 to 10 on theNational Framework of Qualifications

Higher Education programmes from level 6 to 10 on theNational Framework of Qualifications

- Professional Awards

Professional Awards

- Foundation programmes.

These programmes are offered by a range of education providers.
