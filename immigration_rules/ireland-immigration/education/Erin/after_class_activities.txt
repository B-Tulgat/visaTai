# After Class Activities

## Enhance yourEnglish skills

- Enhance your English skills and broaden your learning horizons with ourAfter-Class Workshop Programme! Designed as a free resource for students, these one-hour sessions provide an excellent opportunity to practice and develop your English language skills beyond the classroom.

Led by experienced teachers from our General English Program, workshops cover a diverse range of topics tailored to students’ needs, including IELTS and exam preparation, job interview skills, CV writing, foreign languages such as French or Italian, vocabulary building, pronunciation, conversation practice, and even English through music and songs.Held on different days throughout the week, these workshops are the perfect way to deepen your learning while exploring practical, real-world applications of language. Don’t miss out—join a workshop today!

You can stay up to date with each After-Class by following us on Instagram!​
