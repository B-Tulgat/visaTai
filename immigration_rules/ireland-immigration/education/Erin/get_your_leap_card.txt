# How to getyour  student Leap Card

## Dublin & Cork

The Student Leap Card is the national student travel card. Card holders can get student discounts on fares for Go-Ahead Ireland, Dublin Bus, Luas, Bus Éireann, DART and Irish Rail (Iarnród Éireann) as well as other participating private transport operators in the Republic of Ireland

## To apply for the student leap card
you need to be a part-time student.Courses of less than three months
are not eligible.

## Collection Point

## Dublin

Once the process has been completed you will be given a code which you will need to present in any of the collecting points to be printed.It can be done in universities, Dublin Bus office on O’Connell St. or in Trinity College Student Union.

## Cork

Once the process has been completed you will be given a code which you will need to present in any of the collecting points to be printed.You can collect from the UCC Students’ Union 54 College Rd, University College, Cork
