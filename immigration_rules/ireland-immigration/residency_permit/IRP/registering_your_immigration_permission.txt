# Registering your immigration permission

If you are from a country outside the European Union, UK or Switzerland, and come to Ireland to work, study, live or join family for more than 90 days, you must register with Immigration Service Delivery.

## General Information

### What is registration?

Registration is how we record that you have been given a permission to stay in Ireland and what you can legally do while you are here.
